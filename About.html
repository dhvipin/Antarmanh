Our Approach

मन:स्थली is interpreted as ‘a place within the mind’. The purpose of मन:स्थली thus is to explore the mind in order to analyse and manage mind related issues. There is body and mind interaction in every breath we take but still a dichotomy exists between the two when it comes to handling somatic issues with psychological roots. This initiative aims at spreading awareness and education regarding mental health issues as well as exploring innovative methods to help and manage emotional and behavioral issues in children, adults and elderly alike. Mindscape has similar connotations in English with reference to varying terrain of emotions, cognitions and perceptions and focuses on using media and technology to meditate and discuss on issues of mind. Thus

मन:स्थली ( Dr. Jyoti Kapoor’s Psychiatry clinic ) and मन:स्थली-The Mindscape (Online Psychological, educational and TelePsychiatry) are ventures to offer mental health services to general population not just from a clinical perspective but also from educational and social perspective.

Our Story

मन:स्थली (Mental Health & Wellness Services)

At मन:स्थली, we deeply believe that mental health is not just about diagnosing a condition—it's about understanding the whole person. Our approach is rooted in compassion, empathy, and a commitment to explore and address every issue holistically. We recognize that emotional well-being is complex and influenced by a variety of psychological, social, and biological factors, and thus, we focus on creating a space where individuals feel truly heard and supported. The environment at मन:स्थली is carefully designed to be warm, welcoming, and non-clinical—far removed from the typical sterile settings associated with mental health clinics. This allows our clients to speak freely, without anxiety or hesitation, about their thoughts, emotions, and challenges. We want every individual to feel that they are in a safe space, where vulnerability is met with acceptance, and distress is met with understanding. While we ensure emotional comfort, we also place equal emphasis on the biological and clinical aspects of psychological and psychiatric concerns. Every case is approached with diligence, and each factor—emotional, physiological, or environmental—is thoughtfully examined. Our expert team ensures that any treatment or management plan is not just clinically appropriate but also tailor-made to suit the client’s personal context.just to manage their mental health,.

OUR TEAM

Dr. Jyoti Kapoor

Founder Director | Senior Psychiatrist MBBS, DPM, DNB - Psychiatry (Founder Director)

Dr. Jyoti Kapoor Madan is a well-renowned Psychiatrist in Delhi- NCR. After completing her MBBS from Sardar Patel Medical College, Bikaner, she went on to study Psychiatry at the esteemed Dr. Vidya Sagar Department of Psychiatry, PGIMS Rohtak. With over 18 years of experience, Dr. Jyoti had worked as a Psychiatry Consultant, Researcher, and psychotherapist in her tenure with famous hospitals. Before moving to Gurgaon in the year 2011 she was working as a consultant with Asha Institute of Medical Psychology & Counseling, Hyderabad for 2 years. After having gained extensive knowledge in the evaluation and management of all psychiatric disorders both through pharmacological and nonpharmacological interventions, she is currently working as a senior consultant Psychiatrist at Paras Hospital, Gurgaon and also runs her own mental well-being set up, Manasthali.

Ms. Aishwarya Raj

Clinical Psychologist – M.Phil (RCI Licensed)

Ms. Aishwarya Raj is a licensed Clinical Psychologist with over a decade of professional experience in the field of mental health. She holds a postgraduate degree in Clinical Psychology from Delhi University and an M.Phil in Clinical Psychology from the esteemed Dr. Vidya Sagar Department of Mental Health and Behavioral Sciences, PGIMS Rohtak. Her clinical practice spans diverse age groups including children, adolescents, adults, couples, and families and encompasses a wide range of psychological concerns such as anxiety, mood disturbances, emotional dysregulation, interpersonal difficulties, adjustment issues and personality-related challenges. Trained in evidence-based therapeutic modalities, Aishwarya integrates principles from psychodynamic psychotherapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), trauma-informed frameworks, and Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT). Her work is informed by a strong theoretical foundation, guided by clinical precision, and delivered with empathy and clarity. She offers a reflective and supportive space for individuals at various stages of their psychological journey including those who may be unsure about where to begin, yet feel ready for change. Her focus lies not only in symptom reduction however also in fostering insight, resilience and long-term emotional well-being. Whether navigating transitions, managing chronic stress or seeking a deeper understanding of oneself, her ethos remains: Mental wellness is a lifestyle, an intentional process rooted in awareness, agency and sustained psychological growth.

Ms Ritu Kuhar

M.Sc. (Clinical Psychology) Assistant Psychologist

Ms. Ritu Kuhar is a dedicated and compassionate mental health professional with a strong academic foundation in clinical psychology. She has completed her M.Sc. in Clinical Psychology from CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Delhi-NCR. She brings a reflective and client-centered approach to her work and has gained hands-on experience through diverse internships and clinical exposure. Her areas of interest include personality development and emotional regulation. She believes in blending evidence-based practices with humanistic values to support holistic well-being. She is especially passionate about mental health awareness and community outreach. She is actively involved in psychological assessments and therapy practice.Her aim is to continue evolving as a psychologist by integrating research, compassion, and culturally sensitive practices in her work.

Dr. Shambavi Das

Ph.D. (Indian Classical Music), M.Phil. (Music – Cognitive Musicology), Advanced Certification in Music Therapy, Dual Major in Music & Journalism (USA)

Dr. Shambhavi Das is a TEDx speaker, Indian classical vocalist, and one of India’s leading voices in music therapy. As Founder and Director of the Manonaad Centre for Music Therapy, she blends ancient raga traditions with modern psychological science to promote emotional healing, cognitive development, and mental wellbeing. With a Ph.D. from Delhi University and advanced training in cognitive musicology and therapeutic applications of sound, she has developed evidence-based protocols that support individuals dealing with anxiety, depression, neurodevelopmental conditions, and trauma. She has worked with over 2,000 clients and trained hundreds of practitioners across India. Currently serving as Professor at MGKVP Varanasi and Holistic Head at the Warme Foundation, Dr. Das bridges academia, clinical practice, and public education. Her work has earned her several national honors, including Music Therapist of the Year (2025) and Most Inspiring Woman in Mental Health Innovation (2023). Her approach combines vocal resonance, rhythmic entertainment, and meditative listening, tailored to individual needs. She offers therapy through three centers in Gurugram, including the MAYA Program at Manasthali, creating a sustainable model of holistic care rooted in India’s musical heritage.
