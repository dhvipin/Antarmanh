Psychiatry

Psychiatric Disorders can range from mild anxiety issues to major psychosis and the consequence of such issues leads to poor quality of personal, social as well as physical health. At Manasthali, we ensure comprehensive understanding of the psychiatric issues as per international classification of diseases to make a pragmatic clinical diagnosis and follow a rational approach for management using medicines as well as Psycho education. Clients and care givers are considered partners in treatment and therefore emphasis is on transparency and cooperation.

Liaison

Liaison Psychiatry, also known as Consultation-Liaison Psychiatry, is a specialized service at Manasthali that focuses on the intersection of mental and physical health. Designed for patients receiving medical or surgical care, this service addresses psychiatric concerns that often arise alongside physical illnesses. Our team collaborates closely with hospitals and healthcare professionals to provide timely psychiatric evaluations, emotional support, and mental health interventions within medical settings. By integrating psychiatrists, clinical psychologists, nurses we ensure a comprehensive, multidisciplinary approach tailored to each patient’s unique needs. Liaison Psychiatry is especially effective in areas such as geriatrics, oncology, neuropsychiatry, and in managing medically unexplained symptoms. In addition to direct patient care, we support medical teams through mental health consultations and professional training. This integrated care model improves patient outcomes, shortens recovery time, and enhances overall quality of life.

Assessment helps us understand cognitive, emotional and behavioral functions to build a tailored treatment plan.

One-on-one or group sessions to help manage stress, trauma, depression, or relationship issues.

Specialized care focused on developmental challenges, learning disabilities, and adolescent emotional issues.

Tools and techniques like CBT, relaxation, and biofeedback to manage day-to-day stress effectively.

Evidence-based counseling to address substance abuse, behavioral addictions, and relapse prevention.
