About MAYA

MAYA (Music and Yoga Awareness) is a therapeutic initiative that integrates classical Indian music, yoga, and psychological well-being. Founded by experts in music therapy and mental health, MAYA helps individuals achieve emotional balance, cognitive clarity, and spiritual peace through guided sessions.

Program Highlights

Music Therapy

Healing through raga-based therapy, vocal resonance, and meditative soundscapes.

Yoga & Mindfulness

Integrative yoga practices that align breath, body, and mind for mental clarity.

Emotional Resilience

Evidence-based psychological frameworks to foster emotional strength and insight.
