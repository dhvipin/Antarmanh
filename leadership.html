Meet Our Leaders

Dr. Jyoti Kapoor

Founder Director | Senior Psychiatrist

Dr. Jyoti Kapoor is a renowned psychiatrist with 18+ years of experience, offering holistic treatment through both pharmacological and psychotherapeutic approaches. She leads Manasthali, promoting mental well-being.

Ms. Aishwarya Raj

Clinical Psychologist – M.Phil (RCI Licensed)

Ms. Raj has over a decade of clinical experience. She offers therapy across all age groups using CBT, DBT, and psychodynamic techniques with empathy and insight.

Ms. Ritu Kuhar

M.Sc. (Clinical Psychology) | Assistant Psychologist

Ritu Kuhar specializes in emotional regulation, personality development, and therapy for youth. She integrates evidence-based practices with compassion.

Dr. Shambhavi Das

Music Therapist | Ph.D. Cognitive Musicology

A TEDx speaker and pioneer in music therapy, Dr. Das merges ancient Indian ragas with modern psychology to heal emotional and cognitive disorders.

Dr. Richa Dhamija

Dentist | Clinical Hypnotherapist

Dr. Richa blends dentistry with clinical hypnotherapy for stress-free care. She runs House of Smiles and is a pioneer in dental hypnosis in India.
