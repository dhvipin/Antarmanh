Psychological Assessment

The Psychological services at Manasthali comprise of Psychometric Assessment and remedial measures focusing on basic issues to major classified mental and behavioral disorders. We provide comprehensive psychological screening and assessment for both children and adults. The assessments are:

  • Memory Testing
  • Intelligence (IQ) Testing
  • Learning Disabilities
  • Autism Spectrum Assessment
  • Vocational Assessment, Career and Aptitude Testing, and Interests Test
  • Personality Assessments
  • Couple and Family Assessments
  • Neuropsychological Assessments in case of Dementias, Epilepsy, and Traumatic Brain Injury

    Psychodiagnostic Clarification for disorders like:

  • Depression
  • Mania
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorders
  • Eating Disorders
  • Sexual Dysfunctions
  • Gender Identity Disorders / Gender Dysphoria
  • Personality Disorders
  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Schizophrenia and psychotic disorders
  • Conduct, Behavioural and Developmental Disorders
